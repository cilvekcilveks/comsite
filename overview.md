## 🎨 DESIGN & USER EXPERIENCE

### Visual Design Philosophy
- **Warm & Welcoming** - Colors that feel community-oriented
- **Clear Hierarchy** - Easy to find what you need
- **Mobile-First** - Fully responsive, great on phones
- **Accessibility** - WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- **Fast** - Optimized load times, instant navigation

### Color Scheme (Suggested)
```
Primary: Warm Blue or Green (trust, community)
Secondary: Accent color (warm orange, gold)
Neutral: Clean whites, light grays
Status colors:
  ├─ Green: Active, confirmed
  ├─ Yellow: Pending, upcoming
  ├─ Red: Cancelled, urgent
  └─ Gray: Past, archived
```

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, friendly, readable
- **Body**: Clean, accessible sans-serif
- **Accent**: Slightly larger for important info
